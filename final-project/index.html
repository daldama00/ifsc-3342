Project Questionaire

Who is the target audience?

The main audience is hobbyists in Arkansas and surrounding states. This includes collectors looking for high-end sports cards, TCG players (Pokémon/Magic), and families looking for a fun weekend event in Little Rock.

What are the goals for the site?

My goal is to create a central hub where people can quickly find the schedule and location. I also want to build hype for the event using a modern "Redline" aesthetic that feels more like a professional sports app than a basic website.

How will I gather the information?

I’m gathering details by researching websites vendor lists, checking convention center availability, and looking at previous card show itineraries.

How can I tailor the site to reach the audience?

The way I can tailor the site to reach the audience is by posting on social media about information regarding the current Arkansas Card Show.

Who is responsible for updating the site?

Since this is my final project, I am responsible for maintaining and updating the website. I handle all the CSS styling, HTML structure, and JavaScript functionality to make sure the site stays responsive and clean.

How often should the site be updated?

Ideally, it should be updated monthly leading up to the show. I’ll need to update it weekly to reflect new guest announcements and final tournament brackets.
