Arkansas Premier Card & Collectibles Show

Hey Collectors! Get ready for the biggest hobby event in the Natural State!

Whether you are hunting for that elusive 1/1 sports masterpiece, building your ultimate Pokémon deck, or looking for rare autograph memorabilia, this is the place to be. Join thousands of fellow fans in the heart of Little Rock for a weekend of trading, gaming, and unforgettable memories!

September 18th – 20th, 2026

Show Highlights

👤 Special Guests

Autographs & Q&As. Guests may leave early due to prior obligations or take breaks which may not be subject to any refunds

🃏 100+ Vendors

Sports & TCG Cards. Browse a massive selection of vintage grails, modern hits, and sealed wax. Whether you're hunting for Hall of Fame rookies or rare TCG holos, our diverse lineup of sellers has something for every collection.

🏆 Tournaments

Super Smash Bros & More. Join the battle in Super Smash Bros., Street Fighter 6, and local TCG qualifiers. Daily brackets, professional casting, and massive prize pools for the top finishers.

🍔 Food Trucks

Local Little Rock Eats. Good cards, great games, and even better food. Head outside to the food truck row to enjoy Little Rock’s best comfort food, cold drinks, and local treats in a vibrant festival atmosphere.
